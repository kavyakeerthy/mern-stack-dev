Pinterest is a visual discovery and social media platform that allows users to find and save ideas for various interests, including home decor, fashion, recipes, travel, and more. launched in 2010, the app enables users to create and organize virtual pinboards by saving images, known as "Pins," from the web or directly uploading them. With a powerful search engine and personalized recommendations, Pinterest helps users explore new trends and get inspired. It also serves as a marketing tool for businesses, allowing brands to showcase products and drive traffic to their websites. The platform's user-friendly interface and creative focus make it a popular choice for inspiration and planning.
